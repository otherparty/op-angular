Sustainable Aviation

The bill is part of the FAA's efforts to reduce emissions and promote sustainability in the aviation industry.

Incarcerating Parents

Sen. Ron Wyden (D-OR) recently introduced a bill to Congress that aims to reduce the incarceration of parents of minor children, expectant parents, and other caregivers. The bill would divert these individuals from incarceration and into comprehensive programs providing resources, services, and training to those individuals and their families. The bill has been read twice and referred to the Committee on the Judiciary. If passed, the bill could lead to a decrease in the number of parents and caregivers who are incarcerated.
